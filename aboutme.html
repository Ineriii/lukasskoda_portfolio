Get to know me better

I live in Slovakia and am currently studying graphic design at Piaristická spojená škola Františka Hanáka v Prievidzi. Over the past two years, I’ve gained practical skills in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, as well as foundational knowledge in HTML and CSS, which introduced me to the world of web development.

Looking ahead, I’m excited to expand my skill set by learning Blender for 3D design, Adobe Premiere for video editing, and diving deeper into web development with JavaScript and PHP. Additionally, I have basic proficiency in Office 365 applications such as Word, Excel, and PowerPoint, whFich aids in organizing tasks and creating presentations.

I’m always eager to learn new things and adapt to the evolving world of technology and design trends. My straightforward and honest communication style ensures clarity in collaboration, making every project an opportunity to grow and create meaningful work.
